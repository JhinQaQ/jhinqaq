About me

Hello! My name is Jin Yang, and I am a Master’s student at Syracuse University. Under the guidance and supervision of Prof. Yuzhe Tang, my academic focus and research interest is centered around blockchain security, where I am constantly exploring and investigating ways to enhance the security measures of this innovative technology.

Currently, I am working as a research assistant in FullStack Security Lab (FSSL) at Syracuse University, a dynamic research group that is dedicated to exploring various security challenges and issues in different aspects of technology. I have been a part of this research group since October 2022, and it has been an excellent opportunity for me to apply my knowledge and skills to real-world security challenges and collaborate with other professionals in the field.

Apart from my academic and research pursuits, I enjoy exploring the great outdoors and indulging in my passions for photography and snowboarding. Whether it’s capturing stunning landscapes or shredding down a snow-covered mountain, I find that these activities help me unwind and find balance in my life.
